Frequently Asked Questions

What types of projects does Clarks Consulting specialize in?

We specialize in complex development projects including infrastructure planning, economic impact analysis, environmental assessments, regional planning, and financial feasibility studies for both public and private sector clients. Our expertise spans transportation, energy, water resources, urban development, and environmental policy.

What geographic regions does Clarks Consulting serve?

We serve clients globally with particular expertise in East Africa (Kenya), the United States, Middle East (Qatar), and Pacific regions. Our experience spans multiple continents and diverse regulatory environments, allowing us to adapt best practices to local contexts.

What is your experience with government projects?

With over 35 years of experience, we have worked extensively with government agencies including King County Washington, Washington Dept of Ecology, Montana Department of Transportation, and international governments on major infrastructure and policy initiatives. We understand public sector procurement, reporting requirements, and stakeholder engagement processes.

How do you approach complex economic analysis projects?

We employ a systematic approach: 1) Problem definition and scoping, 2) Data collection and validation, 3) Model development using appropriate tools (IMPLAN, custom econometric models), 4) Scenario analysis, 5) Impact assessment, and 6) Clear reporting with actionable recommendations. We emphasize transparency and methodological rigor.

What is your typical project timeline?

Project timelines vary based on complexity and scope. A standard economic impact assessment typically takes 4-8 weeks, while comprehensive regional planning or environmental impact assessments may require 3-6 months. We provide detailed project plans with milestones during the proposal phase.
